Employee Grievance

A dedicated HR workflow for receiving, investigating, approving, and closing employee grievances without mixing the process into the broader HR dashboard.

Case Intake Interview Logs Approval Signatures Evidence Tracking

What It Solves

This addon gives grievance handling its own controlled workspace so HR teams can track sensitive cases with clearer ownership, status progression, and approval history.

Key Workflow

Employees can submit grievances, HR acknowledges and assigns them, investigators document findings, and managers finalize resolutions with explicit signatures and notification steps.

Core Features

  • Standalone grievance records with priority, confidentiality, deadlines, and involved employees.
  • Assignment workflow from draft through investigation, resolution, closure, or rejection.
  • Interview and investigation tabs with attachment support for supporting evidence and documents.
  • Outcome, disciplinary action, corrective action, and complainant communication tracking.

HR Controls

  • Mail chatter integration with logged status updates and activities.
  • Completed activity feedback copied into interview records for auditability.
  • Investigating officer and HR manager signatures recorded separately.
  • Search, list, kanban, and grouped reporting views for daily case handling.

Compatible with Odoo 19 and designed for organizations that need a clearer and more defensible grievance resolution process.
